Products

PNEUMATIC ACTUATORS

Powerful, compact rack & pinion Pneumatic rotary quarter turn actuators in E & P series. For both the series option available is double acting & spring return type actuators. Apart from standard actuators, various special actuators can be built to suit a wide variety of extreme applications: Half Turn Actuators, 3 Position, high temperature, low temperature, offshore, with different operating media, etc.

ELECTRIC ACTUATORS

Electric Rotary actuators. There are 3 series namely ELS, EL, ELQ. They are available for On/Off applications and also modulation at various voltages and protection classes.

VALVE AUTOMATION SYSTEM

Remote operated valves with Pneumatic or Electric actuators for On/Off and modulating operation with necessary control elements to suit the customers typical requirements. The system is tailor made to meet the requirement of the specific application of the industry. The systems are offered with butterfly, ball, plug or special purpose valves depending on the process condition.

About Us

EL-O-MATIC (INDIA) Pvt. Ltd. Solutions to Process Industries In its wide and deep experience in supporting the growth of the Indian process industry, the Poonawalla Group of Engineering Companies realised the acute need of automatic valve control for the modern process industries, which would ensure safe and effective performance. In 1989 EL-O-Matic (INDIA) came into being, consequently the Indian process industry got international quality Pneumatic Actuators and Valve Automation Systems for valve control, which plays a critical role in its manufacturing process.
